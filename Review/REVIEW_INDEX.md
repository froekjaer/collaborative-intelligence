**Baseline:** v0.1

## Purpose

This directory preserves the original independent architectural reviews used to evaluate the Mission Framework ecosystem before Foundation v1.1.

Original reviewer artefacts are historical evidence. They shall not be rewritten to match later terminology, conclusions or repository state.

## Derived analysis

The lead synthesis is maintained separately under:

```text
Analysis/Baseline-v0.1/
```

The synthesis interprets the corpus. It does not replace, amend or outrank the original reviews.

## Immutability rule

After an original review is accepted into the archive:

- its content is not edited for agreement or consistency
- errors discovered later are recorded in a separate erratum or analysis file
- reviewer metadata and limitations remain attached
- later reviews use a new versioned directory

## Citation rule

Programme decisions should cite both the relevant original review evidence and the synthesis or decision record that interprets it.

Reviewer count is not proof. Evidence quality, scope accuracy and empirical results take priority.
